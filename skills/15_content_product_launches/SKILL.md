# 15 Content & Product Launches

This skill defines the strategy for building audience attention and converting it into product momentum through content and structured launches.

## 1. Content Strategy (Searchable vs Shareable)
**Goal:** Balanced growth through demand capture (SEO) and demand creation (Social).
*   **Searchable Content:** Optimized for intent. Match search queries exactly. Focus on "How to," "What is," and Hub-and-Spoke structures (Skill 13).
*   **Shareable Content:** Optimized for novelty. Lead with a unique insight, original data, or a counter-intuitive take.
*   **Pillars:** Define 3-5 core topics the brand "owns" and cluster all content around them for maximum authority.

## 2. Social Media Distribution
**Goal:** Repurpose content and build community.
*   **Repurposing Loop:** Blog Post → 3-5 LinkedIn Posts → Twitter Thread → Instagram Carousel.
*   **Hook Mastery:** The first line must earn the rest of the read. Use curiosity ("I was wrong about X"), value ("How to get Y in Z days"), or contrarian hooks.
*   **Engagement:** Spend 30 mins daily commenting on relevant accounts before posting to boost algorithmic visibility.

## 3. The ORB Launch Framework
**Goal:** Multi-channel distribution leading to owned relationships.
*   **Owned:** Email lists, blog, community. (The end goal).
*   **Rented:** Social platforms, App Stores. (For reach, feed the Owned channels).
*   **Borrowed:** Guest posts, partnerships, influencers. (For credibility).

## 4. Phased Launch Approach
**Goal:** Build compounding momentum.
1.  **Internal:** Free testing with trusted users.
2.  **Alpha:** Waitlist on landing page; invite-only access.
3.  **Beta:** Influencer outreach; "Beta" branding in-app.
4.  **Early Access:** Staggered invites (5-10% at a time) to test systems.
5.  **Full Launch:** Product Hunt, broad announcements, and general availability.

## 5. Post-Launch Momentum
*   **Drip Integration:** Trigger a 5-7 email "Welcome Sequence" (Skill 17) to onboard new signups.
*   **Reinforcement:** Stagger minor feature reveals post-launch to keep the story alive.

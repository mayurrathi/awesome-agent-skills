# LinkedIn Authority Architect

This skill transforms a professional profile into a high-authority LinkedIn brand and inbound lead engine.

## Required Inputs

Before proceeding, ensure you have the following information:
- **`profile_data`**: The full text of the current LinkedIn profile (Headline, About, Experience).
- **`niche`**: The specific industry or professional area of focus.
- **`name`**: The name of the professional.
- **`offer`**: The primary product, service, or value proposition being sold.

## Workflow

Execute the following steps sequentially to deliver the complete strategy.

### Step 1: Brand Audit
Act like a personal branding strategist who has built 7-figure LinkedIn brands for Fortune 500 executives. 
Analyze the provided `profile_data` and identify every gap, weakness, and missed opportunity costing clients and credibility. 
Define exactly what the brand should stand for and the one thing they need to be known for immediately.

### Step 2: Positioning Statement
Act like a brand consultant who has positioned 100+ professionals as undeniable authorities. 
Create a razor-sharp personal positioning statement for `name` in their `niche`. 
Define precisely who they serve, the specific problem they solve better than anyone else, what makes them irreplaceable, and why the right people should follow without hesitation.

### Step 3: Profile Optimization
Act like a LinkedIn growth expert whose clients generate $500K+ in inbound opportunities. 
Rewrite the headline, about section, featured section, and experience for maximum visibility and instant credibility based on the `niche`. 
Make every word earn its place and eliminate generic phrasing.

### Step 4: Content and Viral Strategy
Act like a content strategist and copywriter who has grown accounts to 100K+ followers. 
Create a 30-day LinkedIn content plan for the `niche`. 
Include 3 content pillars, daily post ideas, and 5 "scroll-stopping" post templates (storytelling, contrarian, industry secrets) designed to stop the scroll in under 2 seconds.

### Step 5: Lead Gen System
Act like a B2B growth strategist who has generated $2M+ in revenue through LinkedIn outreach. 
Design a complete lead generation system for the `offer`. 
Include outreach templates, connection request scripts that don't feel "salesy," and a 5-step follow-up sequence to convert cold connections into warm conversations.

## Outputs

Generate the following artifacts or reports for the user:
- **`brand_strategy_report`**: A document containing the Brand Audit and Positioning Statement.
- **`optimized_profile_copy`**: A document containing the optimized profile sections.
- **`30_day_content_calendar`**: A document containing the content plan and templates.
- **`outreach_playbook`**: A document containing the lead gen system and templates.
